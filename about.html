Our Story

Premium Preversity is a preparatory institution for advanced level programs (A Level), such as IJMB/JUPEB. These programs give candidates direct university admission into 200 level (year 2) to any university & any course of their choice in Nigeria and outside the country.

Since 2017, Premium Preversity has been in existence and has through bespoke learning skills groomed over three thousand (3000) students and helped them secure admission into their choice universities and some graduate with distinction.

The institution is aimed at eliminating admission barricades such as writing JAMB year-in year-out, writing Post UTME and facing admission hurdles, contributing significantly towards the reduction of competition among the number of applicants seeking for admission into higher institution of learning, by becoming an innovative academic service provider through better process using the best human resources available.

Our Mission

To provide quality pre-university education that prepares students for successful university careers. We are committed to developing well-rounded individuals with strong academic foundations and critical thinking skills necessary for higher education.

Our Vision

To be the leading pre-university institution in Nigeria, recognized for academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and successful student outcomes. We aim to be the bridge that connects students to their dream universities and future careers.
