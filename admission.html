Application Process

Step 1: Complete the Application Form

Fill out our online application form or visit our campus to obtain a physical application form. Ensure all information provided is accurate and complete.

Step 2: Pay Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application fee of ₦20,000 through any of our approved payment channels. Keep your payment receipt for reference.

Step 3: Submit Required Documents

Submit all required documents along with your completed application form. Incomplete applications may delay the admission process.

Step 4: Admission Screening

Qualified candidates may be invited for an admission screening or interview. This is to assess your suitability for the program and to provide you with more information.

Step 5: Admission Offer and Acceptance

Successful candidates will receive an admission offer. To secure your admission, you must pay the acceptance fee within the specified timeframe.

Frequently Asked Questions

Both IJMB and JUPEB are advanced level programs that prepare students for direct entry into 200 level in Nigerian universities. The main differences are in the examining bodies and some aspects of the curriculum. IJMB is moderated by Ahmadu Bello University, while JUPEB is a national examination body approved by the Federal Government of Nigeria.

Yes, you can apply while awaiting your O'level results. However, you will need to present the required results before securing admission to the 200-level in your chosen university.

We offer various subject combinations for different disciplines, including Science (e.g., Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics), Social Science (e.g., Economics, Government, Geography), Arts (e.g., Literature, History, CRS/IRS), and Management Science (e.g., Accounting, Economics, Mathematics). The specific combination you choose should align with your intended university course.

Both IJMB and JUPEB programs typically last for 9-10 months, depending on the academic calendar and examination schedule. The programs usually start around November/December and end in June/July.

IJMB certificates are recognized by all Nigerian universities. JUPEB certificates are recognized by all Nigerian universities and some international institutions. Upon completion of either program, you can apply for direct entry into your preferred university.
