PREMIUM PREVERSITY

Premium Preversity is a preparatory institution for advanced level programs (A Level), such as IJMB/JUPEB. These programs give candidates direct university admission into 200 level (year 2) to any university & any course of their choice in Nigeria and outside the country.

Since 2017, Premium Preversity has been in existence and has through bespoke learning skills groomed over three thousand (3000) students and helped them secure admission into their choice universities and some graduate with distinction.

Our Academic Programs

Premium Preversity offers quality pre-university programs to help you secure admission into your dream university.

IJMB Program

Interim Joint Matriculation Board (IJMB) is an advanced level program that prepares students for direct entry into 200 level in Nigerian universities.

  • Direct entry into 200 level
  • Recognized by all Nigerian universities
  • Comprehensive study materials
Learn More

JUPEB Program

Joint Universities Preliminary Examinations Board (JUPEB) is a national examination body approved by the Federal Government of Nigeria.

  • Admission into 200 level
  • Internationally recognized qualification
  • Expert tutors and mentors
Learn More
