JUPEB Programme

JUPEB stands for Joint Universities Preliminary Examinations Board. It is a national examination body approved by the Federal Government of Nigeria that conducts standardized examinations for candidates seeking admission into 200 level in Nigerian universities.

The JUPEB program is designed to prepare students for direct entry into 200 level in Nigerian universities and some international institutions. It is a one-year program that covers three subjects related to the student's desired course of study.

Key points about the JUPEB program:

Duration: The JUPEB program runs for one academic session (9-12 months), typically from September/October to June/July.

Curriculum: Students study three subjects related to their intended university course. The curriculum is comprehensive and designed to prepare students for university-level studies.

Recognition: JUPEB certificates are recognized by all Nigerian universities and some international institutions, making it a valuable qualification for higher education.

Admission Requirements: Candidates must have a minimum of five O'Level credits, including English Language and Mathematics, in not more than two sittings.

Examination: The final examination is conducted by the Joint Universities Preliminary Examinations Board, ensuring standardization and quality across all centers.

Our JUPEB program is designed to not only prepare students for their examinations but also to develop critical thinking, analytical skills, and a strong foundation for university studies.

JUPEB Program Details

Program Structure

The JUPEB program at Premium Preversity is structured to provide comprehensive education in specific subject combinations relevant to students' desired university courses. The program runs for one academic session and includes regular assessments to track progress.

Academic Calendar

  • First Semester: September - December
  • Second Semester: January - April
  • Revision Period: May
  • Final Examinations: June

Program Components

  • Intensive classroom lectures
  • Practical sessions for science subjects
  • Regular tests and assessments
  • Comprehensive study materials
  • University placement counseling

Subject Combinations

JUPEB students take three subjects related to their intended university course. We offer various subject combinations for different disciplines:

Admission Requirements

Basic Requirements

  1. Candidates must possess a minimum of 5 Credits in relevant SSCE subjects, including English Language and Mathematics, achieved in not more than 2 sittings.
  2. Applicants with pending results are also eligible to apply, on the condition that they can present the required O'level results before securing admission to the 200-level.
  3. Upon completion of the JUPEB programme, candidates should obtain the JAMB Direct Entry form to facilitate their admission into Premium Preversity or any other university of their preference.

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form or obtain a physical form from our campus
  2. Pay the application fee
  3. Submit the completed application form along with required documents
  4. Attend an interview (if required)
  5. Receive admission notification
  6. Pay acceptance fee to secure your admission

Advantages of JUPEB

International Recognition

JUPEB certificates are recognized not only by Nigerian universities but also by some international institutions, providing students with more options for higher education.

Direct Entry

Successful JUPEB candidates can gain direct entry into 200 level in Nigerian universities, saving time and resources in their academic journey.

Comprehensive Curriculum

The JUPEB curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation for university studies, covering essential concepts and skills needed for success in higher education.

Smaller Class Sizes

JUPEB programs typically have smaller class sizes compared to traditional university settings, allowing for more personalized attention and better learning outcomes.
