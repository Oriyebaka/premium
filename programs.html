Academic Programs at Premium Preversity

We offer comprehensive pre-university programs designed to prepare students for direct entry into 200 level in Nigerian universities. Our programs are recognized by all Nigerian universities and many international institutions.

IJMB Program

The Interim Joint Matriculation Board (IJMB) program is an advanced level program that prepares students for direct entry into 200 level in Nigerian universities. It is recognized by all Nigerian universities and is equivalent to A-Levels.

Key Features:

  • Direct entry into 200 level
  • Recognized by all Nigerian universities
  • Comprehensive study materials
  • Experienced instructors
  • Regular mock examinations
Learn More

JUPEB Program

The Joint Universities Preliminary Examinations Board (JUPEB) program is a nationally recognized pre-university program that prepares students for direct entry into 200 level in Nigerian universities and many international institutions.

Key Features:

  • Admission into 200 level
  • Internationally recognized qualification
  • Expert tutors and mentors
  • Comprehensive study materials
  • Career guidance and counseling
